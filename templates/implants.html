What is an implant procedure like?

1. Consultation

A comprehensive exam and digital imaging will help to create a personalized implant plan.

2. Surgery

Surgery can be done in one or two appointments depending on your condition.

3. Healing & Checkup

After surgery, we monitor healing and ensure the implant is integrating properly with the bone.

4. Impression

After 3-6 months from the surgery, impressions are taken to design a custom crown that matches your natural teeth.

5. Crown Cementation

The final crown is securely placed, restoring your smile's function, comfort, and appearance.
